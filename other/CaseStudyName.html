Case study: Clearpoint

==>    Their mission is to improve lives through financial education. They have ebooks, videos, quizzes and calculators.
==>     They also try to identify the individual financial concerns and plans to address them
==>     What I learned was that this website was really effective on counseling and guiding people through financial literacy through their website.
==>     This is mainly good for people in debt or new to financial literacy to go learn more through their website as they have a great variety of tools.
