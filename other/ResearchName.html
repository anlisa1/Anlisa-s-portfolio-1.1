Financial Stress correlates to a lower academic performances.

  • Theory of intergenerational correlation with genetic transmission from parent to children in terms of education.
  • Unclear planning also causes a bit of the stress that drags down people's academic performances.
  • Student Debt, Expected Graduation/Student debt, and debt among freshmans all have negative effects on the college student academic performances.





Stress on your current financial situation or stress on your future financial situation relates to lower academic performances

  • Stress on the future relates to perceived stress and expected graduation debt stress.
  • Stress on your current financial situation includes working while studying and juggling between your finances and your living condition.
